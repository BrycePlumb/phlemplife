Who are annuities for?

Annuities are purchased for the purpose of risk management within your portfolio. With an annuity you are protected from market losses and the interest credited from the annuity is not tied to stock market gains. This options is good for individuals who are nearing retirement and are focused on taking risk out of their retirement plans.

What do I need to know about life insurance?

Life insurance is for protecting your family or business when you pass away. The proceeds from a death benefit are tax free and can be used however the beneficiary chooses. Permamnet life insurance can accumulate cash value that can be used for emergencies, supplementing income, and funding a major purchase. Life insurance, either term or permament, is a necassary compenent for optimal finanical health. Explore Life Insurance

Are there options for businesses?

Businesses also use life insurance to prepare for the continuation of a business. Partners in a business can purchase life insurance policies on eachother to create a buy-sell agreement. Businesses also use permament life insurance to create executive bonus plans for their key members.
