Annuities limit the market risk in your portfolio.

This can protect you from losing what you have worked your whole life for.

SPIA

An Single premium immediate annutiy (SPIA) is a product that provides income for life or a certain period of time

FIA

An Fixed indexed annuity (FIA) is a product that earns interest linked to growth in a market index.

MYGA

A multi year gurantee annuity (MYGA) is a product that earns a competitve interest rate for a specific amount of years.
