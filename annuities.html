Annuities

Annuities are long-term investments that can provide a steady stream of income in retirement. They are a popular choice for individuals who want to ensure that they have a reliable source of income in their golden years. At Phlemp & Financial, we offer a range of annuities to meet your specific needs and goals.

Types of Annuities

  • Immediate annuities: Begin providing income immediately after purchase
  • Deferred annuities: Accumulate value over time before providing income
  • Fixed annuities: Provide a fixed rate of return

Not sure which type of annuity is right for you? Contact one of our agents to learn more about your options and get personalized recommendations.
